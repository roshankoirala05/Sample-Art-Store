Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The potrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Dominique Ingres, and
today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three potraits of the Riviére family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Mousee du Louvre, Paris
